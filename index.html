Limitations:
Congressional stock trades are not disclosed to the public in a very accessible way. Senatestockwatcher.com manually enters each disclosure into their database to the best of their ability. This analysis covers only 2020, because that is the only year that has complete data for both the House and Senate.
Congress members do not disclose their total holdings, just individual trades.
Congress members are not required to disclose the exact value of each trade, only the range that it falls in. As such, the precise amount invested can only be approximated based on the disclosed range.
